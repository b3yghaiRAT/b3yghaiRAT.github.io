ABOUT

55 MAIN GULBERG ,LAHORE , PUNJAB ,PAKISTAN

Lahore Grammar School 55 Main’s Sports Society has played an integral role in carrying forward the institution’s legacy in ensuring young women each year receive a well-rounded and holistic education. Having consistently placed emphasis on the importance of sports from a young age, we have successfully provided an avenue to budding athletes from all over Lahore over the course of three previous editions, and cordially invite you to the fourth edition of our Sportsfest, scheduled for the 11th till 13th of November, 2022 As an all girls school, Lahore Grammar School realises how crucial it is for young girls to have a platform wherein they are able to engage in sports, with previous editions of the event having hosted all girls teams from all over Lahore to give them the same level of opportunity as boys teams. 55 Sportsfest aims to be an event where talented individuals are able to get recognition for their skills and learn how to partake in healthy competitions, by equipping them with essential skills needed to become an experienced athlete, including teamwork, self-confidence, responsibility, and self-discipline, which will benefit them in other aspects of their lives as well.

55 Sportsfest this year is delighted to introduce a range of categories; from basketball, baseball, and badminton, to ludo, chess, and gaming — we surely have it all. With an astounding number of sixteen categories, and experienced category heads and co-heads, this sportsfest is sure to provide a memorable experience. By showcasing your athletic capabilities, engaging in a gruelling game of darts, or outlasting your opponents in a competitive round of volleyball, we aim to accommodate delegates with all avenues of interest. At 55 Main, we hope to foster, nurture, and develop the natural talents of every attendee, certainly making this sportsfest one of the most memorable highlights of the year.

THROWBALL

THROWBALL

•Each team will comprise of 7 active players and 2 substitute players.
• 1 match will contain 3 sets with 15 points each,and the sides switching after every set.
•The team that scores the point will serve next the ball cannot touch the net or fall in the deadzone.
•Only 2 steps are allowed while the ball is in the player's hand.
•The players cannot jump while serving, but can when throwing and catching the ball.
•Double touches, fumbles and body touches are not allowed, if while attempting to catch the ball 2 players come into contact with one another, it is concidered a foul.
•Underhand throws are not allowed.
•The teams can take 2 time outs of 1 minutes each during sets.
•A player must serve the ball within 5 seconds of the whistle and must return the ball within 5 seconds of catching it, failure to do so will result in the opponent's points.
•If anyone fails to show up within 15 minutes after the allotted time of his or her match, a walkover willbe given.
•Decisons made by the officials would be final and non-contestable.
