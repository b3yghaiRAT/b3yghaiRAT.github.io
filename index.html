ABOUT


Lahore Grammar School 55 Main’s Sports Society has played an integral role in carrying forward the institution’s legacy in ensuring young women each year receive a well-rounded and holistic education. Having consistently placed emphasis on the importance of sports from a young age, we have successfully provided an avenue to budding athletes from all over Lahore over the course of three previous editions, and cordially invite you to the fourth edition of our Sportsfest, scheduled for the 11th till 13th of November, 2022 As an all girls school, Lahore Grammar School realises how crucial it is for young girls to have a platform wherein they are able to engage in sports, with previous editions of the event having hosted all girls teams from all over Lahore to give them the same level of opportunity as boys teams. 55 Sportsfest aims to be an event where talented individuals are able to get recognition for their skills and learn how to partake in healthy competitions, by equipping them with essential skills needed to become an experienced athlete, including teamwork, self-confidence, responsibility, and self-discipline, which will benefit them in other aspects of their lives as well.

55 Sportsfest this year is delighted to introduce a range of categories; from basketball, baseball, and badminton, to ludo, chess, and gaming — we surely have it all. With an astounding number of sixteen categories, and experienced category heads and co-heads, this sportsfest is sure to provide a memorable experience. By showcasing your athletic capabilities, engaging in a gruelling game of darts, or outlasting your opponents in a competitive round of volleyball, we aim to accommodate delegates with all avenues of interest. At 55 Main, we hope to foster, nurture, and develop the natural talents of every attendee, certainly making this sportsfest one of the most memorable highlights of the year.

CATEGORIES


THROWBALL

•Each team will comprise of 7 active players and 2 substitute players.
• 1 match will contain 3 sets with 15 points each,and the sides switching after every set.
•The team that scores the point will serve next the ball cannot touch the net or fall in the deadzone.
•Only 2 steps are allowed while the ball is in the player's hand.
•The players cannot jump while serving, but can when throwing and catching the ball.
•Double touches, fumbles and body touches are not allowed, if while attempting to catch the ball 2 players come into contact with one another, it is concidered a foul.
•Underhand throws are not allowed.
•The teams can take 2 time outs of 1 minutes each during sets.
•A player must serve the ball within 5 seconds of the whistle and must return the ball within 5 seconds of catching it, failure to do so will result in the opponent's points.
•If anyone fails to show up within 15 minutes after the allotted time of his or her match, a walkover will be given.
•Decisons made by the officials would be final and non-contestable.

BASKETBALL

•Each team consists of 5 active players and 2 substitute players.
• The match will be 20 minutes long consisting of 4 quarters of 5 minutes each.
•A player who commits 5 fouls in one match will be disqualified and will need to be substituted in 5 seconds.
Fouls include:
1)If a player double dribbles or simultaneously dribbles with two hands. 2) Tripping/pushing the opponent. 3) Slapping the ball before it leaves the opponent's hand while they shoot.
•Nails must be kept short.
•No equipment such as guards, casts or braces that may lead to injuries can be used.

FUTSAL

•Each team consists of 5 active players on the field and 2 substitutes.
• The match will be 20 minutes long with 15 minute halves.
•A player will be given a yellow/red card if they commit a foul.
•A penalty will be given if handball is committed within the box.
•Penalties/ freekicks will be awarded to the opponents if:
1)The player argues with the referee. 2)The player touches the ball with their hands. 3)The player trips or kicks their opponents.
•Shin pads are recommended.
•Cleats (studs) are not allowed.

TUG OF WAR

•A team consists of 8 active players and 1 substitute player.
• Each match will consist of 3 sets.

BADMINTON

•Each game consists of 3 sets with 11 points,10 being the match point.
• Semi-finals and finals will consist of 3 sets with 21 points, 20 being the match point.
•Serve is decided through coin toss.
•The player who wins a set gets to start the serve in the next round.
•For singles, if the shuttlecock lands outside the first white line it is considered out.
•If anyone fails to show up within 15 minutes after the allotted time of his or her match, a walkover will be given.

TABLE TENNIS

•The service is decided through a service toss.
• The game consists of 3 sets. The first player to reach 11 points wins, however they must win by a two point margin.
•Each player must serve two points in a row after which the server is changed.
•After each set, the players switch sides.
•If the ball touches the net during service and lands on the other side, it is a service fault and is redone. Two service faults result in a point for the opponent.
•Double touches, fumbles and body touches are not allowed, if while attempting to catch the ball 2 players come into contact with one another, it is concidered a foul.
•Underhand throws are not allowed.
•Hold the ball in your open palm, behind your end of the table and toss at least 6 inches straight up

DODGEBALL

•Each team consists of 6 active players and 2 substitute players.
•A match will consist of 3 sets of 3 minutes each.
•Teams may only substitute between sets because of injuries.
•In order for the hit to count, it must be a direct hit. If the ball bounces and then hits, it does not count.
•If the opponent catches the ball they can either opt to save the life or remove the opponent whose ball they caught.
•Shots on the head are not counted.
•If the player steps outside the lines, they are out. If the player catches the ball while their foot is on the line, they are out.

TENNIS

•The ball must be served from behind the baseline, between the center and sideline.
• If the serve falls outside the service box/hits the net, the player is allowed to serve again.
•If the serve is faulted twice, the opponent gains a point.
•The players must change positions after every point and ends are changed after every odd game.
•The knockout round consists of 8 games while the final round will be of one set (6 games)
•If both players have won 6 games each and the set is tied, a tie breaker will be played where the player who gains 7 points, with a 2 point lead. wins the game and the set.
•The tie breaker game will only take place when both players win one set each in the finals •A foul will lead to a point being awarded to the opponent in case of the following:
1)Touching opponents ground, net, posts, or any person in the game
2)Hitting the ball twice before it has crossed the net
3)If the ball hits or touches anything or is carried by the player
4)Throwing the racquet and/or verbal abuse.
5)Double fault
6)A double racquet touch
8)The player is unable to return the ball in the opponent's court before it bounces twice
9)The player comes in contact with the net
10)The ball goes beyond the baseline/ hits the net

•Players must bring their own equipment.
•This is a knockout tournament.

LUDO

•Basic ludo rules will be applied for all matches.

CAROM

•Matches will not have any set time limit.
• Standard game rules will be followed.

BASEBALL

•Matches are based on knockout basis and the basic chess rule will be applied.
• The queens will be worth 9 points.
•The rooks will be worth 5 points.
•The bishop/knight will be worth 3 points.
•The pawn will be worth 1 point.
•Total time allotted for each match will be 30 minutes.
•3 fold repetition equals a draw.
•The 50 moves rule equals a draw.
•In case of draw/stalemate, a rematch will take place for the same amount of time.

CHESS

•Matches are based on knockout basis and the basic chess rule will be applied.
• The queens will be worth 9 points.
•The rooks will be worth 5 points.
•The bishop/knight will be worth 3 points.
•The pawn will be worth 1 point.
•Total time allotted for each match will be 30 minutes.
•3 fold repetition equals a draw.
•The 50 moves rule equals a draw.
•In case of draw/stalemate, a rematch will take place for the same amount of time.

ATHLETICS

•Basic athletics rules will be applied

VOLLEYBALL

•Each team consists of 6 active players and 2 substitute players.
•Serves must be done from behind the line and can be done underhand or overhead.
•The match will comprise 3 sets, 15 points each.
•The team who wins the point will serve.
•There is a maximum of 3 hits per side. A player cannot hit it twice consecutively (a block is not considered a hit.)
•If 2 players come in contact with the ball simultaneously, it is considered 1 play and these 2 players cannot partake in the next 1 play. •Any part of the body is allowed to come in contact with the ball as long as you are not directing it.

STRONGWOMEN/MEN

•Push up competitions will be based on a one minute challenge; how many the player can successfully complete within a minute.
•1 match will take place between 2 participants.
•It will be ensured that participants are completing the push-ups in the universal manner with, a straight back, bending elbows, arms forming two 90-degree angles.
•The arm wrestling will be a knockout tournament.

DARTS

•Standard Dart rules will be applied.
• This will be played in a knockout style.

SCAVENGER HUNT

• Each team can consist of 5 players from the same school.
•Basic Scavenger Hunt rules will apply.
